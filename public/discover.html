WHAT ARE ADD-ONS?

SAP meets the base requirements of all industries & the companies operating within the said industries. However, the difference in requirements between any two given businesses is stark. And that's where Add-ons, certified for SAP, come into play. Add-ons are created according to the needs and requirements of a particular industry or business, as and when required. They are a great way to expand the functionalities of your SAP Solution.
